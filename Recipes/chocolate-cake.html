Chocolate Cake

Burnt chocolate cake recipe

Very simple chocolate cake recipe that probably will not actually yield any cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg. of chocolate
  • 500 g of flour
  • 200 g of butter
  • 1/2 tbs of baking powder

Preparation

  1. Chop and melt the chocolate
  2. Mix the melted chocolate in with flour, butter and baking powder
  3. Put your mix in the oven at 200 degrees celcius for 3 hours
  4. Bake until very burnt and throw out when cooled down.
